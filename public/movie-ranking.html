My Best Movie's Choices

TOP 3 Movie List Here :

The Maze Runner

"Maze Runner" is a surprisingly good film, with mystery, action and sci-fi with wonderful special effects. The plot has many surprises and the young cast has great performance.

Who Am I

"Who Am I" is a German cyber-crime mindfak thriller. The Ending is mindblowing.

The Hitman's Bodyguards

A lot of good action, a lot of bad language and a few laughs. I guess I expected maybe a few more laughs, but this movie is fun, just very typical. I like Ryan Renolds here.
